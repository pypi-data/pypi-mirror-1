Sweepy - a very basic minesweeper clone written in Pylons

Choose your difficulty:

  • Easy Peasy: 5x5, avg. 10% bombs
  • Easy-ish: 7x7, avg. 15% bombs
  • A bit harder: 8x8, avg. 20% bombs
  • More challenging: 9x9, avg. 25% bombs
  • Hard: 12x12, avg. 30% bombs
  • Ultra hard: 15x15, avg. 40% bombs
  • Mental: 20x20, avg. 50% bombs
