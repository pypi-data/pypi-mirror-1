Installing RedirectionTool

  To install RedirectionTool, either uncompress the product into
  your zope/Products directory or link it there, e.g.::

    ln -s /path/to/installation /path/to/zope/Products

  or use the egg Products.RedirectionTool if you use buildout or another
  egg based setup.

  Install it on your site through the Plone ''Add-on Products' control panel.
